## Public Domain Status of The Urantia Book

The original English text of *The Urantia Book* is in the **public domain** in the United States.

- **Michael Foundation v. Urantia Foundation**, 320 F.3d 1093 (10th Cir. 2003): The United States Court of Appeals for the Tenth Circuit upheld that the renewal copyright on *The Urantia Book* was invalid, placing the original English text in the public domain.
- The copyright on the English text formally expired in 2006.
- Non-English translations may still be under copyright by their respective translators or publishers.

## Preservation & Archival

### Blockchain Archive (Arweave)

The complete English text of *The Urantia Book* is permanently stored on the Arweave network in two formats:

| Format | Arweave Transaction ID |
|--------|----------------------|
| Structured JSON | `3RLvenzfBZ60GlOygqu6DtlbEhOWldQSqjGuMSVOg9I` |
| Plain Text | `0YgTs2SJmIkCHxouFPdN-pWFon0ZnhjYQpg7F2DW70o` |

Gateway URLs: `https://{gateway}.arweave.net/{txId}`

Arweave provides immutable, decentralized storage — once written, content cannot be altered or removed.

### OpenTimestamps (Bitcoin-Anchored Proofs)

All MIT LICENSE files and this LEGAL.md have been timestamped using [OpenTimestamps](https://opentimestamps.org), which anchors SHA-256 hashes to Bitcoin transactions. This provides cryptographic proof that these license dedications existed on or before the timestamp date.

| File | SHA-256 Hash |
|------|-------------|
| `LEGAL.md` | See `LEGAL.md.ots` (self-referential hash omitted) |

Proof files (`.ots`) are stored alongside their source files. Verify with: `ots verify <file>.ots`
